Case file — healthcare platform

A clinic that runs on two logins.

One for the person who isn't feeling well. One for the person who helps them. MediCare pairs a calm patient-facing booking site with a full clinical dashboard — requests, schedules, charts, messaging, and payouts — built as one connected product.

01 · Patient journey

Booking a doctor shouldn't feel like paperwork.

Four short screens carry a visitor from "I don't feel great" to "I have an appointment" — no forced sign-up wall, no dead ends.

/doctors
STEP 01

Meet the team

Browse specialists by name and speciality before committing to anyone.

/book
STEP 02

Book in one form

Pick a department, a date, and describe the symptoms — an account can wait.

/login
STEP 03

Sign in, or don't

Return with an email and password, or skip straight through with Google.

/register
STEP 04

Tell us who you are

One intake form captures what a doctor will actually ask at the first visit.

02 · Provider dashboard

Everything a doctor's day touches, in one screen.

Dr. Edalin Hendry's dashboard is the other half of the product — where a booking request turns into a kept appointment, a chart note, and eventually a paid invoice.

/requests
Requests

A queue, not an inbox flood

New bookings wait here — video call, audio call, or in person — until a doctor accepts or declines them.

/appointments
Schedule

Upcoming, done, and cancelled

One filterable board, searchable by patient name — not by memory of who booked what.

/dashboard
Overview

The day, at a glance

Patients today, revenue this week, and who's next — the numbers a doctor actually opens the app to see.

/patients
My patients

One profile, whole history

Appointments, prescriptions, records, and billing for each patient, filed under a single name.

/availability
Availability

Open hours, set in seconds

Add or remove slots day by day — no back-and-forth emails to hold a time open.

/messages
Messages

Every channel, one thread

A doctor replies with text, a voice note, or a video link — the patient sees one continuous conversation.

/payouts
Payouts

Pick a rail, get paid

Stripe or PayPal, configured once — then every completed transfer is tracked underneath.

/invoices
Invoices

Every visit, on paper

Each booking becomes a printable invoice, linked back to the appointment it came from.

/profile
Profile

Their own credentials, their call

Bio, education, clinics, and business hours — a doctor edits all of it without asking an admin.

03 · Under the hood

Two audiences, one system of record.

The hard part of a platform like this isn't either portal on its own — it's keeping them honest with each other. A request accepted on the doctor's side has to show up as a confirmed booking on the patient's side, on the same clock.
