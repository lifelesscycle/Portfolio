01 — PIPELINE

From raw upload to trusted output

A fixed sequence, purpose-built so a reviewer never has to guess what stage a file is in or what's expected of them next.

01

Upload & ingest

Files land in object storage the moment they're dropped in — image, audio, text, or video — and are fingerprinted for dedup before anything else touches them.

02

Model annotation

Each file runs through its modality's annotation model. Output is stored alongside a confidence score, not treated as ground truth.

03

Sampling engine

Rather than reviewing everything, the sampling engine pulls a statistically representative subset — weighted toward low-confidence and edge-case output — into the review queue.

04

Human review

Reviewers work the queue through a modality-specific interface: correct, confirm, or reject, one item at a time, with full context alongside the model's original call.

05

Progress & export

Reviewed batches roll up into agreement rates and per-modality accuracy, ready to export or feed straight back into the next training run.

02 — MODALITIES

One review interface, four kinds of judgment

Image, audio, text, and video each demand a different reviewing motion — so each gets its own workspace, not a generic form.

Image

Bounding boxes, segmentation, and tags overlaid directly on the frame for a fast accept / correct / reject pass.

0 ANNOTATED

Audio

Waveform-synced transcripts with word-level timing, so a reviewer can catch a misheard word without relistening to the whole clip.

0 TRANSCRIBED

Text

Classification, sentiment, and entity labels reviewed inline against the source passage, side by side.

0 ANALYZED

Video

Frame-by-frame scrubbing over tracked objects and event annotations, built for reviewing motion, not just moments.

03 — INFRASTRUCTURE

Built to stay honest about its own state

The platform reports on itself the same way it reports on the data — no silent failures, no annotation queue nobody's watching.

Platform Health
A live check across every dependent service, refreshed on demand — so a stalled reviewer knows immediately whether it's their queue or the platform.
Sampling Engine
Runs on a schedule or on demand, drawing new review batches with weighting rules that stay tunable per modality.
MinIO
S3-compatible object storage holding every raw file and annotation artifact, self-hosted so nothing leaves the review environment.
Redis
Backs the review queue and the auto-refresh cycle, keeping the dashboard's numbers current without polling the database directly.
